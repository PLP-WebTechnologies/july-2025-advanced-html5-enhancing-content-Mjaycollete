Key Terms in Dental Therapy

Dental Therapist
A licensed oral health professional who provides preventive and basic restorative dental care.

Fluoride Treatment
The application of fluoride to the teeth to strengthen enamel and help prevent decay.

Sealants
Thin protective coatings applied to the chewing surfaces of molars to prevent cavities.

Oral Hygiene Education
Teaching patients proper brushing, flossing, and diet habits to maintain good oral health.

Scaling and Polishing
A procedure to remove plaque and tartar from the teeth, especially below the gum line.
